# 🎬 PRODUCTION DOCUMENT: NEON BUSHIDO
**Project Type:** Cyberpunk-Samurai Short Film  
**Total Estimated Runtime:** ~50 seconds (4 Scenes)  
**Target Aesthetic:** Hyper-realistic Neo-Noir / High-Contrast Cinematic

---

## 🟦 SCENE 1: THE LONE WALK (Establishing)
**Theme:** Melancholic honor in a decaying, high-tech future.
**Setting:** Narrow Shinjuku-inspired alleyway at toxic orange/violet dusk.
**Key Action:** The samurai walks through neon crowds, hand on his sword hilt.

> **FINAL VIDEO PROMPT:**
> *Cinematic 8k video, ultra-detailed. A lone traditional Ronin samurai with a straw hat and weathered kimono walking through a rain-slicked cyberpunk Tokyo alleyway at dusk. Deep violet and toxic orange sky. High-contrast neon cyan and magenta lighting reflecting in oily puddles. Volumetric steam rising from street vents. Low-angle tracking shot following the samurai from behind. 35mm anamorphic lens, shallow depth of field, realistic raindrops, cinematic atmosphere, moody neo-noir aesthetic. Heavy film grain, hyper-realistic textures.*
> **(Recommended: Kling 1.5 High Quality)**

---

## 🟥 SCENE 2: THE INTERSECTION (Confrontation)
**Theme:** Tension and the clash of tradition vs. technology.
**Setting:** A dense "Cyber-Shanty" intersection during a heavy thunderstorm.
**Key Action:** Three cyber-thugs block the path; the samurai prepares his blade.

> **FINAL VIDEO PROMPT:**
> *Hyper-realistic 8k cinematic video. A tense standoff in a cyberpunk Tokyo intersection during a heavy thunderstorm. A lone samurai in a traditional straw hat and kimono faces three cybernetic thugs with glowing red ocular implants. Close-up on the samurai's weathered hand flicking a katana hilt. Flickering white neon lighting, harsh shadows, and red rim lights. Slow-motion rain falling through the frame, volumetric fog, and occasional lightning flashes illuminating the scene. 100mm macro lens focus pull, gritty neo-noir aesthetic, highly detailed textures of wet leather and polished steel.*
> **(Recommended: Luma Dream Machine 2.0)**

---

## ⚡ SCENE 3: ONE STRIKE (Climax)
**Theme:** Lethal, instantaneous grace; "Speed-ramping."
**Setting:** The intersection erupts into a blur of neon and steel.
**Key Action:** A single, fluid "Iaijutsu" draw that neutralizes the threat in a flash of light.

> **FINAL VIDEO PROMPT:**
> *Hyper-kinetic 8k action sequence. A lone samurai in a traditional kimono performs a lethal "Iaijutsu" sword draw in a cyberpunk Tokyo rainstorm. Speed-ramping effect: the samurai moves in a blur of motion, leaving a trail of electric blue neon sparks. Side-profile tracking shot with extreme motion blur in the background. Close-up on the steel blade cutting through falling raindrops in slow-motion. High-contrast lightning flashes, volumetric rain, and realistic water splashes. Cinematic neo-noir aesthetic, hyper-detailed textures of wet fabric and sparking cybernetics.*
> **(Recommended: Runway Gen-3 Alpha Turbo)**

---

## 🌫️ SCENE 4: THE FOG OF DAWN (Resolution)
**Theme:** Resigned peace; the weight of tradition.
**Setting:** A small Shinto shrine tucked between megastructures at a hazy dawn.
**Key Action:** The samurai cleans his blade and disappears into the morning mist.

> **FINAL VIDEO PROMPT:**
> *Cinematic 8k resolution, slow-paced ending. A lone samurai in a weathered kimono stands before a small stone Shinto shrine hidden between towering cyberpunk skyscrapers at dawn. Soft blue hour lighting with a hazy golden sunrise. Volumetric fog and rising steam. Close-up on the samurai cleaning a katana with silk. Wide shot showing his silhouette walking away into a thick white mist. Moody, melancholic atmosphere, hyper-realistic textures of wet stone and glowing neon bokeh in the far distance. 50mm lens, 4k resolution, epic scale.*
> **(Recommended: Kling 1.5 or Luma 2.0)**

---

### 🔊 MASTER SOUNDSCAPE NOTES (For all scenes)
- **Music:** Haunting low-fi synthwave mixed with traditional *shakuhachi* flute and *koto* strings.
- **Ambience:** Constant low-frequency city hum, rain sizzle on neon, and distant digital chatter.
- **Key Foley:** Rhythmic *clack* of wooden sandals, metallic *shiiing* of the blade, and the electronic *zap* of failing implants.
